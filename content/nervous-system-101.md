---
title: Nervous System 101
type: framework
---
The nervous system is always asking one question underneath everything: am I safe right now?

In a settled state we can think, connect, and create. Under threat we shift into fight or flight, feeling anxious or wired. If threat feels inescapable, we can drop into shutdown, feeling numb, foggy, or collapsed.

None of these states is bad; they are protective. The work is learning to notice which state you're in and gently guiding yourself back toward safety through breath, movement, and connection. (Sample content for demonstration.)
