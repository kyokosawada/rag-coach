---
title: 4-7-8 Breathing
type: breathwork
---
The 4-7-8 breath is a calming pattern that lengthens the exhale, which tells the body it is safe to slow down.

Empty the lungs. Breathe in quietly through the nose for a count of four. Hold the breath for seven. Exhale completely through the mouth for eight, with a soft whooshing sound. Repeat for four rounds.

It is especially helpful at night, when the mind won't settle. Keep the counts relative to your own comfortable pace. (Sample content for demonstration.)
