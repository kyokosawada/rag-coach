---
title: The Reframe Ladder
type: framework
---
The reframe ladder is a step-by-step way to loosen a painful thought without forcing false positivity.

Start by writing the raw thought exactly as it sounds, such as "I always ruin things." Next, question it: is this completely, always true? Then find a more accurate middle statement, like "I made a mistake here, and I've also handled hard things well before."

The goal isn't to leap to "everything is wonderful." It's to climb one honest rung toward a thought that is both truer and kinder. (Sample content for demonstration.)
