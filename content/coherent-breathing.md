---
title: Coherent Breathing
type: breathwork
---
Coherent breathing means slowing the breath to about five to six breaths per minute, where the heart and breath find a steady rhythm together.

Breathe in gently through the nose for a count of five, then out for a count of five. Keep it smooth and effortless, with no holding. Continue for five to ten minutes.

Unlike sharper techniques, this one is about sustained ease rather than a quick reset. It builds a calmer baseline over time. (Sample content for demonstration.)
