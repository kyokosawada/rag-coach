---
title: Building Self-Trust
type: framework
---
Self-trust is built the same way trust with another person is: through small promises kept over time.

Choose one tiny commitment you can absolutely keep today, such as drinking a glass of water in the morning or a two-minute walk. Then keep it, and quietly notice that you did.

Every kept promise, however small, is evidence to yourself that you are someone who follows through. Start smaller than feels impressive; consistency matters more than size. (Sample content for demonstration.)
