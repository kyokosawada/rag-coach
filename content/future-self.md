---
title: Future Self Visualization
type: framework
---
The future-self practice uses imagination to make a wiser, calmer version of you feel real and reachable.

Close your eyes and picture yourself one year from now, having moved through what you're facing. Notice how she stands, how she speaks, what she has let go of. Ask her one question and listen for the answer.

Then bring back one small thing she would do this week. The point is not to predict the future but to borrow her perspective today. (Sample content for demonstration.)
