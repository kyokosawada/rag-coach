---
title: Loving-Kindness
type: meditation
---
Loving-kindness meditation warms the heart by silently offering goodwill, first to yourself and then outward.

Bring a hand to your heart and repeat softly: may I be safe, may I be well, may I be at ease. Then picture someone you love and offer them the same words. Widen the circle to someone neutral, someone difficult, and finally all beings.

It is a gentle antidote to self-criticism and resentment. Let the phrases be sincere, even if the feeling is faint at first. (Sample content for demonstration.)
