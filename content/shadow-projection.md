---
title: Owning Projections
type: shadow-work
---
A projection is when something we can't accept in ourselves gets seen loudly in other people instead.

Think of someone who really irritates you and name the exact quality that bothers you. Then ask honestly: where does a version of this live in me, even in a small or hidden way?

This isn't about blaming yourself. It is about reclaiming energy that gets lost in judging others, and softening toward both them and you. (Sample content for demonstration.)
