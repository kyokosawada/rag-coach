---
title: Morning Intention
type: meditation
---
Setting a morning intention gives the day a quiet center to return to.

Before reaching for your phone, sit for a minute and take three slow breaths. Ask yourself: how do I want to feel today, and what is one small way I can honor that?

Choose a single word or short phrase, such as "steady" or "open," and carry it with you. When the day pulls you off course, the word is a way back. (Sample content for demonstration.)
