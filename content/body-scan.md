---
title: Body Scan
type: meditation
---
A body scan is a slow tour of attention through the body, meeting each part exactly as it is.

Lie down or sit comfortably. Bring attention to the feet, and slowly move up through the legs, hips, belly, chest, arms, and head. At each place, simply notice sensation without trying to change it.

If the mind wanders, gently guide it back to the last place you remember. This practice builds the skill of feeling the body instead of only thinking about it. (Sample content for demonstration.)
