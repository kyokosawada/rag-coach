---
title: Breath of Fire
type: breathwork
---
Breath of fire is an energizing practice of rapid, rhythmic breathing through the nose, powered by the belly.

Sit tall. Breathe in and out through the nose in equal, quick pulses, letting the exhale snap the navel toward the spine and the inhale happen on its own. Start with 20-30 seconds, then rest.

Use it when you feel foggy or flat and want to feel awake and alive. Skip it if you are pregnant or feel lightheaded, and always return to a normal breath afterward. (Sample content for demonstration.)
